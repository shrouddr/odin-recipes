Buffalo Wings

Delicious and crispy Buffalo Wings that are great for any occasion!

These resturant style appetizers are a surefire way to bring some spice to a Game Day or celebration!

What you will need to bring the best wings to your Game Day!

  • Chicken Wings! (Duh), you can use either fresh or thawed frozen wings or drumettes.
  • Baking Powder. This is how we will make the Buffalo Wings extra crispy
  • Salt, because why not?
  • Garlic Powder. Garlic will add a little zest to the wings before being tossed in the buffalo sauce.
  • Butter (salted or unsalted, your preference). This will help add a creaminess to the buffalo sauce.
  • The sauce can be entirely up to you, go as hot as you would like, or no heat if you prefer. I recommend Frank's Red Hot Sauce.
  • Sugar. The sugar will help the buffalo sauce caramelize and thicken and make your wings dripping with sauce!
