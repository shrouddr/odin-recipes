Chicken Asado

Pollo Asado, or Chicken Asado is a delicious Mexican or Cuban-style recipe that can be combined with many different sides.

Perfect for easy meal-prepping or a quick dinner!

What you will need

  • The Chicken - It is best to use boneless and skinless thighs, as this will result in a juicier taste.
  • Olive Oil - This will help distrubute flavor all around the chicken.
  • Orange Juice - Orange juice will add a citrusy flavor, and tenderize the chicken.
  • Lime Juice - This will provide a zesty flavor to the chicken, and will help balance out the orange juice.
  • Garlic - An essential ingredient that helps bring depth to the chicken.
  • Achiote or Paprika powder - Both will bring a nice smokiness to the entire dish.
  • Oregano - a common herb used in Latin American cuisine, it gives the chicken an earthy flavor.
  • Cumin - A crucial spice that brings a warming flavor.

How to make!

  1. Add your chicken and all ingredients into a large bowl. Toss everything until the chicken is fully coated in the juices, and seasonings.
  2. Add your olive oil to a large nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Once the oil is hot, add your coated chicken to the pan.
  4. Cook the chicken for five minutes, and then carefully flip, and cook for another five minutes.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and continue cooking your chicken. Flip the chicken occasionally until fully cooked.
  6. Serve!
