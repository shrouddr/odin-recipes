Chicken Parmesan

An easy-to-make Chicken recipe that can be combined with a pasta base for more flavor!

Great for after a long day of work, or to spice up your meal-prepping!

What you will need

  • Four skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper for taste
  • Two large eggs
  • One cup panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Two tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup prepared tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella, cut into small cubes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese
  • Two teaspoons olive oil

How to put it all together

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees F or 230 degrees C.
  2. Season chicken thoroughly with the salt and pepper. Evenly coat both sides of chicken breast with flour.
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl and set aside for later.
  4. Mix bread crumbs and 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese in a bowl, set aside for later.
  5. Dip chicken breasts in beaten egg, then transfer to bread crumb mixture and get both sides.
  6. Heat 1/2 cup of olive oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat. Cook chicken in hot oil until golden. It will finish cooking in the oven.
  7. Transfer golden chicken to a baking sheet. Top each breast with two tablespoons of tomato sauce. Layer each chicken with equal amounts of mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, and provolone cheese.
  8. Any remaining Parmesan can be sprinkled over top, and drizzle each breast with 1/2 teaspoon of olive oil.
  9. Bake in the oven until the cheese is browned and bubbly, and the chicken breasts are no long pink in the center. About 15-20 minutes.
  10. Serve!
